Our Products

Anant India's water solutions are designed to be accessible and affordable for a wide range of consumers.

RD - RO
The specialized RD RO is used for highly polluted waste water treatment / reuse with high efficiency and can handle high TDS, COD, BOD in the effluent.
M.E.E - Multi Stage Effect Evaporator
A multiple-effect evaporator (MEE) is a system used in industrial settings for the evaporation of liquids, typically to concentrate a solution.
CRP - Caustic Recovery Plant
A CRP Caustic Recovery Plant is a facility used in various industrial processes, particularly in industries where caustic soda (sodium hydroxide) is used extensively and needs to be recovered for reuse
 ETP - EFFLUENT TREATMENT PLANT
An ETP, or Effluent Treatment Plant, is a facility used by industries to treat the wastewater (effluent) generated during their manufacturing processes before it is discharged into the environment.
